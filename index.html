About

This is an academic credential verification solution implemented on blockchain technology, a technology that made bitcoins possible. This solution reduces the process that takes several days into a few seconds!

Whether you're a student looking to expedite your transcript verification process, or a university waiting for other university to send the transcript of a particular student, this platform is the perfect starting point!
